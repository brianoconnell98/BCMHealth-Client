Support for Breast Cancer Patients

Here at BCMHealth we want to offer our patients and physiotherapists the opportunity to chat to each other on the application. We have restricted this to registered members whereby you can chat to a fellow patient or else to one of our registered physiotherapists here at BCMHealth. We believe that the mental health of all of our users is as important as the physiotherapy ye receive so the opportunity to chat with others is something we firmly believe in.

Click below to head over to our live messaging chat portal whereby you can chat to any of our registered patients/physiotherapsists.
